(Reflection) Công việc mới và những điều nó học được

The higher a position a person has, the more diligent and effortful they are than others.
  • Start working earlier than others.
  • Finish your work day later than others.
  • Always take advantage of every second and every minute to work.
  • Be dedicated to what you do.
Carefulness and meticulousness are prerequisites for success for Japanese people
  • Be careful with every period and comma
  • Be careful about each uppercase or lowercase letter
  • Correct spelling or not
  • Is it as per the contract....
High EQ is really, really important
  • It's not natural for others to help you. Always express gratitude when receiving help both in words and actions.
  • Be considerate and encourage others in the right way.
Prefer diligence before idleness, unless you esteem rust above brightness.
